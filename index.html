Fort Collins Water Quality Analysis

Home

Welcome to the analysis of Fort Collins, CO water quality. The analysis focuses on the turbidity measurements of the raw water(source water) and
the finshed water, the water that runs throughout households. Turbidity is the measurement of the clarity of water, essentially how dirty the
water is. Measuring turbidity is important because the finished water is supposed to contain little to none in order to pass the allowed level.
